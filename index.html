Chris L. Barker

Chris L. Barker is an Assistant Professor of Economics and Finance at the Carter College of Business at Harding University. Chris received his undergraduate degree in Accounting from Harding in 1993. He also earned a Master's degree in History in 2009 and a Master's in Business Administration in 2016 from Middle Tennessee State University (MTSU). In 2025, he earned a Ph.D. in Economics, also from MTSU. While working on his doctorate, he was a Research Fellow with the Political Economy Research Institute (PERI).
